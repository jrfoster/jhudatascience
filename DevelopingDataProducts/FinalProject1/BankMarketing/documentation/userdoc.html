Introduction

This application allows the user to view the effects of different variables on the predicted likelihood of success of a marketing campaign.

Details

Prediction Input
In the panel on the left, you can choose which values to use in the prediction:

  • The outcome of the last marketing campaign
  • The number of contacts to the customer during that campaign
  • The number of contacts to the customer during the current campaign
  • The customer's age
  • The customer's average yearly balance (euros)

Prediction Rendering
As you make selections in the left panel, a prediction, which is a probability of success, is generated and displayed in two ways in the panel on the right. First, a color gradient scale is shown to represent the probability, between 0 and 1, with lower values on the left and increasing to the right. The actual prediction is represented as a black dot on that scale. Additionally, the actual predicted probability is displayed as a reference.
